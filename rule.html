Welcome to Ecospherehub Puzzle!

Here are the rules for playing the image-based puzzle:

  1. Click on a puzzle piece to select it.
  2. Click on another adjacent piece to swap them.
  3. Continue swapping pieces until the image is correctly assembled.
  4. You can click on the "Shuffle" button to shuffle the puzzle pieces if you get stuck.
  5. Have fun and enjoy the challenge!
